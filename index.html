About Us (Hey There!)

GOALS brings together literature and the art of oration, combing knowledge with flair. It endeavours to arm its members with the communication skills necessary in today's environment and broaden their horizons. We are a group of like minded people with our common interests for any form of poetry, literature, debate, GDs and the list goes on. We were established in 2016 and our progress has been tremendous since then. Hope to see you join our bandwagon.

Activities

We try to conduct as many activities we can to keep the flames within us ignited! Events such as writing articles on burning issues, group discussion, publishing poetry and stories are regular and keeps us updated with contemporary issues while instilling a competitive spirit. And we also keep a prize money for most of the events (extra pocket money while learning never hurts :p).


  




Library

We have our own library with 180+ books and the count is ever growing. The collection covers everything from thriller, horror, mystery to romance and is a book-lover's paradise. Come and get immersed in the vast expanse of man's best friend, yes, books!
